The studio

A film studio, redefined.

Rizz Group AI was founded in 2025, when AI video tools reached a point where they could be used for real cinematic work.

Every project is shaped by directors with decades of experience in cinematography and filmmaking, using a production model that removes traditional limitations while preserving craft. The result is work that feels more considered, more elevated, and often beyond what conventional production can deliver.

Our films have been recognized at the $1M AI Film Award (Top 5, 2025, presented by 1 Billion Followers Summit and Google Gemini) and as a finalist at the Luma x Cannes Lions Dream Brief 2026. We've created commercial work across energy, gaming, fintech, longevity and consumer brands.

A new way of making films. A higher standard of what they can be.
